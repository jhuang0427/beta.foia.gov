Snapshot Reports provide detailed data on each agency's FOIA activities. These reports include metrics from:
 
• Annual FOIA Reports - popular statistics and facts from each agency's most recent Annual FOIA Report that is available on FOIA.gov, and
• Quarterly Data Reporting - key statistics on the administration of the FOIA at each agency, and updated as each quarter of the current fiscal year ends.

 

Please select the link below to view a list of federal agencies and explore each agency's snapshot report. For full data sets from agency Annual FOIA Reports, please visit the Data page.
